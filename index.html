About Satira Media & PR

A boutique public relations and communications agency in Lagos, Nigeria, established to propagate a brand and provide creative communication solutions. We ensure that our clients are held to high regard and provided with the right strategies to keep their target audience glued to their activities and projects. The goal is to carefully steer our clients’ key messages in the desired direction and facilitate strategic partnerships that will enhance their commercial value.

Vision

To become the one-stop agency for providing creative PR and communication solutions.

Mission Statement

To ensure that our clients are provided with result-oriented strategies and solutions to PR and communication issues in a creative, dynamic, and professional way.

Services

What I Do?

PR Advisory and Ideation

We always endeavour to give our clients tailor-made solutions to meet their various needs. We have created several bespoke concepts that set our clients apart from their competitors, and generated engaging strategies that will showcase the brand as effective and different.

Digital Relations

Your digital public relations is also an extension of your brand. We help you connect with your online audience and provide strategic counsel through social media audits, influencer engagements, and integrated digital content.

Reputation and Crisis Management

Reputation and crisis management are one of our areas of specialization. With our expertise in these areas, we will ensure that your brand’s reputation is upheld, well-received among consumers and the target audience, and conflicts that may arise are resolved.

Media Relations

We are able to provide proactive media services for our clients, deploy strategic information/data that can reach their target markets on various digital and traditional media platforms. Our strong relationship with the media has constantly prevented the circulation of unfavourable news for our clients as our media partners keep us in the loop of any negative information that might be making rounds before it’s published.

Thought Leadership PR

As a mark of a proactive agency Satira Media and PR will propose ideas that will distinguish and position your brand’s key executives in leading and shaping conversations in your industry across Africa.

Community Relations

Satira Media & PR has worked with a few individuals and organisations to sustain goodwill within their community. Through the various CSR outreaches that will be created by your organisation, we will increase the conversations among people in various communities towards seeing your brand as one who takes great interest in positively affecting the needs of the community.

Planning, Execution/Activation

We have a top-notch event planning and execution ability that we use to add value to our clients. We have the ability to start a campaign, event or PR activity from the idea to the activation stage. As such, we have a full account managing mechanism in leading projects to full completion, delivering on client’s briefs and business objectives. We have delivered the projects of our clients on time, to budget and to quality.
